TIMEPIECES



We carry a diverse selection of premier watch lines in our store, from Shinola, Breitling, Omega, Bell & Ross, Oris, Frederique Constant, Hamilton, Longines, and Victorinox.

Swiss- and American-made pre-owned and historic watches, railroad pocket watches, military wristwatches and the finest made pieces in the world all hold top billing at Watchworks. Our desire is that every item purchased by our clients be handed down to the next generation.
